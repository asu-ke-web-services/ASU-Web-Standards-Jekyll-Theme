9 Heros #heros

Heros are used at the top of pages to highlight the page name, a description of the content, and an image to accent the page.

9.1 Mobile Hero #heros.mobile-hero

Go mobile to view this section:

Example

Global Sustainability

Improving human well-being and ensuring social equity for present and future generations

<div class="override">
    <div class="hero-mobile" style="background-color: #1e73be;">
        <h1 class="pane-title" style="">Global Sustainability</h1>
        <div class="pane-content" style="">
            <p>Improving human well-being and ensuring social equity for present and future generations</p>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
